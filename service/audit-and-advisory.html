Audit and Advisory

Audit assists in the establishment of ‘best practices’ which in turn leads to sound internal controls within the company. Audit also aids in enhancing the efficiency in operations which leads to performance improvement and profit of the company.

How it helps

  • Adds value in solving the issues faced by businesses by providing insight on the effectiveness of governance, risk management, and internal control processes.
  • Post-audit recommendations help in fortifying controls, enabling efficient bookkeeping and building proficient accounting practices.
  • Depicts a clear picture of the prevailing financial situation of the company and provides valuable insights on future scenario which can prepare companies to handle upcoming conditions in a much better way.
  • Helps in gaining investor confidence as audit report is a valuable tool for financial institutions and investment houses to estimate the valuation of a business firm.

  • At Penny Worth Consultants, our expert professionals leverage hands-on experience to provide your company with the support and assistance necessary to enable you to better fulfill your business mission. Supported by our value-added services and consistency in service delivery, we help our clients move to the next level of business goal.

Operational Audit

Our operational audit services involve systematic evaluation of the overall functioning of an organization, including all internal processes and procedures. We help our client companies achieve optimal utilization of the available resources to maximize profits. The services are customized to the needs of the business of clients and help checking the efficiency of operational structures. Operational audit services at Penny Worth Consultants focuses on the streamlining and optimizing business resources, key processes, procedures, and system, to improve productivity, efficiency and effectiveness of the operation.

Investigative Audit

As part investigative auditing services we use a variety of methodologies to detect discrepancies in operations, financial scenario, and all other key aspects of businesses. We provide expert opinions in implementation on control and enforcement mechanisms. As part of investigative audit, we perform the below..

1. Examination of financial and operational processes in the organization

2. Provide expert opinions on misappropriation and fraud.

3. Help with enforcement mechanisms and anti-fraud plan.

4. Identify any gaps in the functioning of internal control systems

5. Due diligence to identify any risks involved in mergers and acquisitions, investments and other business activities.

Management Audit

At Penny Worth Consultants, management audit involves systematic examination of decisions and actions of the management to enhance the effectiveness of management actions. As part of an elaborate and effective process, it involves the review of organizational objective, policies, procedures, structure, control and system. The reviews help in checking the efficiency of the management over the activities of the Company. Our management audit services concentrate on the inefficiencies and weaknesses of all the functional areas such as sales, inventory, production, purchase, personnel, finance, administration, etc. We evaluate and recommend ways for improving efficiency in all the concerned areas.
