Human Resources (HR) & Payroll Solutions

Competent HR & payroll management can be a difficult, resource-draining task since it requires a wide variety of skills. They are inherently complex as they involve a wide range of activities that are to be meticulously performed in a timely manner keeping compliance and danger of reputation damage in mind.

While the activities seems apparently simple like defining salary structure, monthly calculations, maintenance of muster, reconciliations, etc., they are highly challenging due to the intricacies of legislation and the need to streamline operations while reducing costs.

HR Solutions

Optimal management of Human Resources requires ready templates for different stages of the process. At Penny Worth Consultants, we help businesses of all sizes manage all HR processes efficiently. We offer carefully structured and designed solutions based on a long history of successfully managing the HR operations for startups and established firms. Our solutions can be customized to meet your organizational requirement and preferences.

Some of our key HR service offerings include:


  • HR consulting & advisory
  • Employee Policies
  • Hiring Modules, Employee Onboarding and Exit Processes
  • Records and Formats
  • Performance Management Processes
  • Employee Compensation Structuring
  • HR Budgets

The Payroll Process

Payroll processing involves a whole range of activities from tracking attendance, to bank details of each employee, deductions, bank uploads, salary slip, timely and accurate calculations with optimal tax implications and other compliances. The vast nature of activities involved call for a sizeable and dedicated team. Since businesses with less number employees incur higher payroll administration, they opt for payroll outsourcing. Our Payroll experts take care of components like:


  • Basic pay, HRA, rent allowance
  • Gratuity, TDS and other tax obligations
  • PF & accurate employee salary calculations, deductions and filings in compliance with all the payroll laws
  • Employee life cycle management

Payroll Administration

At Penny Worth Consultants, we are equipped with dedicated and experienced staff with adequate exposure in efficient and confidential payroll administration.

We offer comprehensive payroll administration services include:


  • Payslip generation according to the need of individual clients
  • Management reporting in all necessary aspects including summaries and staff costs
  • Verification of Employee’s IT declarations
  • IT returns filing for employees
  • IT, PT, and ESI statutory reports filing with concerned authorities
  • All statutory payments administration including for EPF, ESI, PT and TDS
  • All statutory forms preparation

Services at Penny Worth Consultants ensure seamless communications between HR and payroll system. The seamless integration gets rid of the requirement to communicate changes across HR and payroll. Any updates made by HR are made available to payroll electronically. This translates to saving time in both departments.
