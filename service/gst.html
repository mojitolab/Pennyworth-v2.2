Goods And Service Tax (GST)

Audit assists in the establishment of ‘best practices’ which in turn leads to sound internal controls within the company. Audit also aids in enhancing the efficiency in operations which leads to performance improvement and profit of the company.

A new, multistage tax structure introduced by Government of India, Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an umbrella tax which replaces a slew of indirect taxes such as central excise duty, duties of excise, additional duties of excise, additional duties of customs, special additional duty of customs, CESS, state vat, central sales tax, etc. GST, however is still in an evolution stage and presents a lot of new challenges to businesses from time to time. In order to ensure that your business can overcome these challenges and ensure that day to day transactions are carried out in a complaint yet profitable manner we provide a slew of services:

GST Advisory Services

We ensure that your business is equipped to cope up with the requirements of the ever-evolving GST law and thus avoid any losses which may occur later due to non-compliance related issues.

GST Compliance Services

We at Penny worth help your business with the umpteen GST compliances on a routine business thus reducing the time spent on such non- core activities and ensuring accurate filing of returns which reduces litigations in the long run.

GST Representation Services

In case of receipt of any notices from authorities we provide appropriate replies and also represent your case against authorities at various forums such as Tax Officers, Appeallate Commissioner, Tribunal ...etc...
