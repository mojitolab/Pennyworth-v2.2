Virtual CFO – Need of the hour

  • March 21, 2020

We often observe an increase in the morale of the company, especially tightly staffed start-ups, when the company reaches a goal that seemed so far away from the beginning. Tackling these difficult goals while maintaining high team spirit and managing finances would be a huge burden on the CEO.

This is where the virtual CFO comes into the picture. Now, you might be wondering: What is a Virtual CFO?

A vCFO or Virtual Chief Financial Officer is an outsourced service provider offering high skill assistance in the financial requirements of an organization, often when requested by the company rather than a full-time hire. Some of the services a virtual CFO provides are:

  • Financial planning
  • Financial reporting
  • Financial risk management
  • Cost management
  • Debt reduction


The mushrooming of the start-ups in India, and a rapid increase in their numbers over the last few years made the need for a Financial officer more demanding than ever. But, why a Virtual CFO? The benefits of having a virtual CFO are:

  • Cost control: You have better control of your revenue without hiring a full-time CFO. Perhaps one of the biggest benefits of hiring a virtual CFO. You get the services you need, when you need them from an expert without bringing in the complexities of the salary management, employee benefits, and other terms into the picture.
  • Diverse expertise: Since you have the choice of who, when, and where to hire a CFO from, you can pretty much vary the expertise depending on the need. Therefore, reducing the revenue spent and bringing in very diverse advice benefitting your company.
  • Faster growth: When you have the power of hiring according to the need while minimizing your expenditure, you experience faster growth. While tackling all challenges of the technical-financial balance of your company with the help of a CFO.
  • Better insights: Transparency and insights are the true features for the possibility of success of the choices you make. After all, your gut can’t always lead to success, it is oftentimes followed by unfortunate consequences. You need to make data-driven decisions. Virtual CFOs provide you with exactly these! They have huge insights on your situation and help you move toward that goal you’ve made for yourself.
  • Customized for your needs and budget: The state of your company now might be very unique, you need someone who understands that and leads you toward your goal. This is very compatible when you hire a Virtual CFO. The solutions are customized for your needs and budget.


Now that you know what a virtual CFO is and some of the benefits of having one. The immediate question that strikes you is, Does my business need a Virtual CFO?

You make the call if you need one or not. The reason why you won’t need one is because it is not yet necessary or an outsourced arrangement is not the best fit. Either way, you need to have a clear and stable understanding of what the financial status of your company looks like right now.

You may need a CFO when:

  • You’re experiencing a faster growth: As your company changes phase, where you get more and more clients, employees, vendors, etc but not yet at a phase where you can afford hiring a full-time CFO. You might need a virtual CFO for placing the systems in place for the seamless transformation of your company between phases.
  • Your revenue is looking unfavourable... but you don’t know why?: All the companies have their highs and lows, like life. A virtual CFO can help you look through these numbers that are pretty foggy and make them crystal clear for you, so you make the right decisions and you know why the finances have been unfavourable. A virtual CFO helps you take the necessary steps to straighten the financial ship.
  • Your business is getting complex: Especially as the workforce increases and the load just keeps on piling up while the numbers are too foggy to recognize anymore.
  • You lack the financial understanding needed to make decisions: For starting a company, one doesn’t really need a degree in finances. We understand that. We walk you through the financial state that your company is in right now, and help you make sound decisions - big or small.
