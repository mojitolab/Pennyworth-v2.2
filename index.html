Have Questions?

Do I need a consultant?

When your business is growing, a consultant can keep up to date with new taxations structures and laws which can be vital to the company's profitability. Having an oversight can be key for your business.

What is the best form of organization for me?

This is a very case specific need. Proprietorship, Partnership, Private Limited and Limited Liability Partnership firms have it's own adventages. You may need to consider your industry requirements too.

What is a virtual CFO?

A virtual CFO offers high skill assistance in financial requirements of an organization, just like a chief financial officer does for large organizations. A virtual CFO is an outsourced service provided by us.

What is an audit?

An audit is an independent examination of financial information of any entity that analize expenses, profits and every financial element of your company. It is a smart idea have your company audited sometime.

How do I register for GST?

Registering for GST has become easy. There is a simple filing of an application that is required and can be done online these days. The important thing is to have your company registration and all documents in order.

How can I save tax?

Tax can be saved in many ways. From managing expenses to investing in the right places, one has many options available today. With the proper guidance and consulting, you may find it easy to save tax.
